Grading Criteria

Your performance in this course will be evaluated based on the following components:

  • Participation: 20% (including discussions and interactive sessions)
  • Assignments: 40% (weekly assignments based on the course modules)
  • Project Work: 30% (a final project encompassing all stages of design thinking)
  • Final Quiz: 10% (to assess your understanding of the key concepts)
